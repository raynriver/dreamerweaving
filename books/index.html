Guide to exploring nature:

We start with demonstrating respectful interest for Nature.

Then we earn access to information directly from Nature, by asking direct questions, as we explore the information pouring to and through us as communication from Nature.

As we consistently show up maintaining this growing relationship, we eventually earn access to the experience of the planet's relationship with the rest of the cosmos.

Once you understand the way natural elements like the wind and animals make sense of communication, you are better able to be a custodian of your own energetic impact on the world within and around you, making you a better custodian of the natural world.

By reading this book, you are beginning to send a signal to the natural world that you want to enter into a better relationship.

Green Witch by Arin Murphy-Hancock
